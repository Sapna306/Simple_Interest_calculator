# Simple_Interest_calculator
A Simple Interest Calculator is a tool used to determine the interest earned or paid on a principal amount over a fixed period at a given rate of interest, without compounding.
Formula for Simple Interest
The standard formula is:
[ \text{SI} = \frac{P \times R \times T}{100} ]
Where:
•	SI = Simple Interest
•	P = Principal amount (initial sum of money)
•	R = Annual interest rate (in %)
•	T = Time period (in years)
